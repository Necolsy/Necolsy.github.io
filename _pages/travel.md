## Travel, slowly

I love planning a route almost as much as taking it. After years of study in Israel, South Korea, and Singapore, travel has become a way to understand places beyond their skyline: their terrain, climate, food, and the rhythms of ordinary life. Given the choice, I will usually take mountains, water, forests, and open horizons over a city checklist.

## Places I have explored

**Asia and the Middle East**
Singapore, Israel, Thailand, Malaysia, Taiwan, South Korea, Indonesia, Japan, and Turkey.

**Europe**
The Netherlands, Finland, Ukraine, France, Italy, and Spain.

**North America and Oceania**
The United States, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand.

The list is still growing. I hope to keep finding landscapes that feel genuinely different from everything I have seen before.
