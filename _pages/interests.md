## Beyond research

Research is only one way I make sense of the world. Outside the lab, I am happiest when planning a trip, immersed in a console game, or working with a camera.

### Travel and route design

I enjoy building the itinerary as much as the journey itself. I have explored Singapore, Israel, Thailand, Malaysia, Taiwan, South Korea, Indonesia, Japan, Turkey, the Netherlands, Finland, Ukraine, France, Italy, Spain, the United States, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand. Nature usually wins over a city checklist: mountains, coastlines, forests, and open horizons are always worth a detour.

### Console games and serious play

I enjoy console games both for their worlds and for the questions they can raise. I was an equal-contributing author of a CHI Extended Abstract on how the death-themed game *Spiritfarer* can help players cope with loss.

### Amateur modelling and photography

When time allows, I take part in amateur modelling and photography. It is another way to pay close attention to light, composition, place, and the stories held in a single image.
